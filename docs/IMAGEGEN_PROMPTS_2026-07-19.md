# INKMADE image generation prompt set

Mode: built-in `image_gen`.

## Shared fit-series prompt

Use case: `photorealistic-natural`. Vertical 4:5 premium e-commerce fit photograph for INKMADE. One adult model, framed so the face is outside the image; seamless warm-gray studio backdrop; matte plain black garment with no logo and no print; softbox from upper left; neutral color balance; realistic cotton, seams, folds and skin; restrained analog grain. No office, production, architecture, props, text, watermark, glossy fabric, plastic skin, warped anatomy, duplicated limbs or extra fingers.

Variants:

1. Classic T-shirt — regular fit, 220 GSM, balanced shoulder, sleeves above elbow, front view.
2. Oversize T-shirt — 240 GSM, dropped shoulder, wide sleeves, relaxed silhouette.
3. Polo — relaxed black pique, clean two-button placket, subtle three-quarter stance.
4. Sweatshirt — 320 GSM, relaxed crewneck, ribbed cuffs and hem.
5. Hoodie — 340 GSM oversized pullover, hood down, kangaroo pocket, no drawstring.
6. Cap — rear three-quarter head crop, six-panel cotton twill, curved brim and rear adjuster, face hidden.
7. Tote — black 320 GSM canvas tote on a model in bone top and dark trousers, face outside frame, bag silhouette and handles unobstructed.

## Shared macro-series prompt

Use case: `product-mockup`. Square 1:1 luxury e-commerce macro, soft raking studio light, deep black detail without crushed shadows, realistic fibers and construction. No people, text, logo, print, production equipment, office, tools, watermark or impossible stitching.

Variants:

1. Polo — pique collar, two-button placket, buttonholes and knit texture.
2. Hoodie — rib cuff, hem and kangaroo-pocket edge, 340 GSM brushed cotton.
3. Cap — cotton-twill panels, eyelet, rear strap and antique-brass adjuster.
4. Tote — canvas weave, top hem, parallel handles and box-cross reinforcement stitching.
